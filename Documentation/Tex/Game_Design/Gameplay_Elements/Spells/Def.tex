\subparagraph{Level 3}                                                                                                                        
Medium spells \\                                                                                                                              
\begin{tabular}{ p{3cm}p{14cm} } \hline  
	\specialcell[p]{\textbf{Finite} \\ \textbf{Incantatem}         \\ \includegraphics[width=2.5cm]{../Pictures/Gameplay/Spells/Icon/Finite_Incantatem_spell_icon.png}} & \begin{tabular}{p{14cm}} \textit{Terminates or blocks spell effects in the vicinity of the caster.} \\ \hline \textbf{Dispel}: Any spell of 3rd level or lower on the target ends. For each spell of 4th level or higher on the target, make an ability check using your spellcasting ability. The DC equals 10 + the spell's level. On a successful check, the spell ends. \textbf{Counter}: You attempt to interrupt a creature in the process of casting a spell. If the creature is casting a spell of 3rd level or lower, its spell fails and has no effect. If it is casting a spell of 4th level or higher, make an ability check using your spellcasting ability. The DC equals 10 + the spell's level. On a success, the creature's spell fails and has no effect. Dispel: At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, you automatically end the effects of a spell on the target if the spell's level is equal to or less than the level of the spell slot you used. Counter: At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the interrupted spell has no effect if its level is less than or equal to the level of the spell slot you used. \end{tabular} \\ \hline  
	\specialcell[p]{\textbf{Episkey}         \\ \includegraphics[width=2.5cm]{../Pictures/Gameplay/Spells/Icon/Episkey_spell_icon.jpg}}           & \begin{tabular}{p{14cm}} \textit{Used to heal relatively minor injuries, such as broken bones and cartilage.} \\ \hline As you call out words of restoration, up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range regain hit points equal to 1d4 + your spellcasting ability modifier. At Higher Levels: The Healing increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 3rd. \end{tabular} \\ \hline                                                                                             
\end{tabular}

\subparagraph{Level 8} 
Supreme spells \\
